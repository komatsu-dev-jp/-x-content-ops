# Legal and Platform Risk Policy

## High Risk Expressions

Do not use:

- 絶対勝てる
- 誰でも稼げる
- 勝率爆上げ
- 必勝
- 攻略法
- 最強
- 革命
- これだけで収支改善
- 期待値だけで勝てる
- 他アプリより確実に優秀

## Safer Expressions

Use:

- 判断を見直す
- 記録で振り返る
- 感覚に頼りすぎない
- 迷いを減らす
- 続行・撤退を後から確認する
- 実戦中の判断ログを残す

## X Policy Risk

Avoid:

- duplicate posts
- excessive hashtags
- unrelated hashtags
- artificial engagement
- automated likes
- automated replies
- automated follows
- browser automation

## Screenshot Risk

Before posting screenshots, check:

- no third-party copyrighted material
- no store names unless intentionally disclosed
- no personal information
- no user IDs or account names
- no competitor UI copied directly
- no unreleased internal details that invite cloning

## Gambling Context Risk

PachiTracker must not encourage reckless gambling.

The product should be framed as:

```text
記録によって判断を振り返るためのツール
```

not:

```text
パチンコで稼ぐためのツール
```
